Works, but Mini.

I've been at it for a minute! This page is a quick one column timeline of sorts. These are just a few of my favorite jobs since I started really getting into design some seven-so years ago.

2021: National Park Diorama Card

A challenging foray into a 3D design makes for an interesting adventure.

2019: Clipper App

It's the prehistoric year of 2019. Clipper has zero mobile presence. What could a mobile app potentially look like?

2019: 811 Live

An elementary exercise: create an app concept and sell it.

2019: Hourglass

An example of taking client ideas beyond expectations.

2018: Enfield branding

How do you make a personal brand that's not 100% personal? It's a bit of a riddle.

2017: Reclamation branding

A group deals with the fallout of a schism and needs a new identity on the double.

2017: Library Signage

The school library gets specialized posters to mark genre shelves.

2017: Onward branding standards

A call for graphic design submissions leads to a district wide competition winner.

2017: Turning Points

A friend gets into publishing his work on the internet and needs an album cover. It's the start to the nice occasional instance of freelance work.

2016: Personal Water Bottle

A long lasting investment gets a long lasting paint job.
